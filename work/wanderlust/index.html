Description

Wanderlust is an international yoga events and festivals company coming up with an ambitious project including redesign, back–end migration and storytelling.

Our task—a team of three developers working remotely—was to develop the front-end code, migrate individual Drupal sites to Wordpress and create a storytelling engine for their team of editors.

And prepare the site for traffic peaks of 50.000 visitors / day when tickets go sale.
