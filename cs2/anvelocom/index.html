anvelocom

anvelocom is a local e-commerce startup. As a first step they've invested in their online presence with this new website.

Services

services
logo, responsive web design, front-end development, and back-end development
technologies
wordpress, php, html5, css3, jquery, sass, and compass

mobile first design

mobile first design

having a strong mobile presence is essential for every e-commerce company. The best approach is to start with a responsive website then move up to a mobile app.

clean and strong style

clean and strong style

since anvelocom products look uniform we've choose a clean style to stay out of the visitor's way and let them focus on the product instead.

complex filters

complex filters

anvelocom has hundreds of products related to each other. we've created a set of interconnected filters via a Wordpress plugin.

the algorithm was a challenge due to the dynamic nature of the filters: editors can add/remove anytime a filter without additional programming needs.

focus on images

focus on images

e-commerce websites are selling first of all images. following this principle the product page uses the largest image possible to help customers to decide.

designed for men

designed for men

the primary target audience of Anvelocom are mid-age men looking for adventure. with this design we've managed to stay masculine.

the logo

the new logo was created following the clean and strong principle. it is clearly distinguishable both on screen and on paper where Anvelocom places lots of advertisements.
