An Approach To Web Design

This is a case study to present an effective approach to web design using industry best practices.

TLDR;

  1. Design around the message
  2. One screen at once
  3. Five elements on a screen
  4. Uniform design elements
  5. Industry standards

1. Design around the message

Every website sells something. A book, a blog post, a subscription, a photo — an information.

The first step starting the design is to identify what the website sells, how this item feels, and how people buying them will feel about.
