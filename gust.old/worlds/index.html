Worlds

There is an outer world, shiny, and projected from above. It’s currency is light, spotlight, fame.

It is built on a huge mass, chaotic dark matter, fueled by just simple attention without focus — waste for more.

Corruption, in Latin, is continuous never ending destruction.

And there is the underworld — never sure but always, and more living — where time is measured in nows.

There is no currency here, everything is free, forgettable, replaceable and changeable.

Here less and constraints are the fuel and small steps mean everything.

The underworld is the last shadow of the matter — beyond comes the twist lighting the shine — again — from above.
