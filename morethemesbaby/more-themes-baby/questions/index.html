Who Are You?

We are an emerging studio crafting WordPress themes with new aesthetics.

With more than ten years WordPress experience, dozens of themes created for clients worldwide, and as members of a distinctive design club—now we do distill this knowledge into themes available for the large public.

Why You’ve Started This Business?

At the end of 2017 WordPress seemed to be a winner. With around 30% of all websites worldwide running on WordPress, and growing, WordPress demonstrated high resilience and agility since it was founded.

And their recent move to latest web development trends and best practices — Calypso and Gutenberg — assures they won’t render themselves obsolete.

As WordPress becomes a de-facto standard in publishing / content management systems there is a need for better, more modern, even avantgarde themes — this is where More Themes Baby steps in.
